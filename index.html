About Me

Physician & Financial Professional

With over 20 years as a practicing physician, I understand the demands, risks, and high-stakes decisions busy professionals face while building wealth. The long hours, the delayed gratification, and building wealth while managing a demanding career. My experience as a real estate investor sharpened my focus on cash flow, liquidity, and navigating market cycles. Today, as a financial wellness professional, I bring these perspectives together to serve busy professionals in their wealth building journey.

My goal is to support your financial wellness through protection-oriented strategies that work alongside growth assets, helping add resilience, flexibility, and long-term efficiency to your overall financial strategy. When aligned properly with your life stage, goals, and risk tolerance, protection can serve as a foundation that allows growth to work more efficiently, especially through uncertainty. This is not to replace what you may already be doing well, but to complement it.

What I Focus On

  • Long-term growth with risk management
  • Access to liquidity, even during market downturns
  • Tax-efficient strategies for high earners
  • Living benefits (long-term care, critical illness, income protection)
  • Legacy and estate planning support for busy families
