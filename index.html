Services Provided

Let's get you started on the path to financial security.

Life Insurance

Protection that works while you're living and leaves a legacy when you're not

Life insurance is more than a death benefit. When structured intentionally, it becomes a cornerstone of financial security, liquidity, and long-term planning and supplemental retirement income for individuals and business owners.

We design individual and business solutions that align with your income, family responsibilities, and stage of life including:

  • Term insurance for cost-effective protection during peak earning and family-raising years
  • Permanent insurance for long-term protection, cash value growth, and living benefits, tax benefits, liquidity and flexibility, even when markets are down
  • Business succession and key-person strategies to protect companies from unexpected loss
  • Executive bonus and retention plans that reward and retain top talent in a tax-efficient way
  • Final expense solutions

Our approach focuses on protection first, while maintaining flexibility, growth and tax advantages so your coverage adapts as your life and business evolve.

Retirement Planning & Annuities

Custom Roadmaps for Growth and Income Security

Retirement planning isn't about guessing, it's about building a clear, adaptable roadmap that balances growth, protection, and predictable income. We help busy professionals and families transition from accumulation to confidence.

This includes:

  • Personalized retirement income strategies aligned with your timeline and lifestyle goals
  • Strategies that complement, not replace your existing 401(k), 403(b), 457, TSP, or investment accounts
  • Annuities designed for growth, protection, or guaranteed income, depending on your needs
  • Personalized supplementary retirement plans using permanent life insurance to safeguard traditional retirement accounts

Our focus is on helping you retire on purpose, with safeguards against market volatility, longevity risk, and unexpected life events.

Cash Balance & Pension Plans

Advanced tax strategies for high earners and business owners

For professionals and business owners looking to accelerate retirement savings while managing taxes, cash balance and pension plans can be powerful tools.

These plans are designed to:

  • Allow significantly higher tax-advantaged contributions than traditional retirement accounts
  • Markedly reduce taxable income, leading to huge savings on taxes
  • Create predictable retirement benefits
  • Support both individual professionals and growing businesses

We help evaluate whether these strategies align with your cash flow, business structure, and long-term goals, ensuring the plan enhances flexibility rather than creating future constraints.

These plans are not one-size-fits-all. We coordinate closely with your CPA and advisors to ensure each strategy is compliant, sustainable, and aligned with your goals.

Family Estate, Business Succession & Legacy Planning

Protect What You've Built. Preserve What Matters.

Your life's work deserves a thoughtful transition, not uncertainty. Whether you're planning to sell, pass on, or gradually exit your business, we help you protect both enterprise value and family wealth.

Estate and legacy planning is not just about documents; it's about protecting people, preserving values, and ensuring the life you've built continues to serve those you care about most.

Our legal partners provide an educational complimentary consultation that helps you understand:

  • How your family, assets, and business interests are currently exposed
  • What happens to your income, business, and responsibilities in the event of incapacity or death
  • How to transition wealth and ownership intentionally, rather than by default

For Families

  • Ensuring access to liquidity when it matters most
  • Reducing stress, confusion, and financial burden during difficult times

For Business Owners
